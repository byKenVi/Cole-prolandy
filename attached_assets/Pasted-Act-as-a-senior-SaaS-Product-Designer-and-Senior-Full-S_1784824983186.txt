Act as a senior SaaS Product Designer and Senior Full-Stack Engineer.

Implement the following UX improvements with a strong focus on speed, usability, and a polished user experience. Keep the existing design system and codebase consistent. Avoid unnecessary refactoring.

## Contractors

### 1. Inline wallet top-up (Highest Priority)
When a contractor doesn't have enough balance to accept a lead:
- Don't redirect them to the Wallet page.
- Open a compact modal/sheet directly from the lead detail.
- Display:
  - Current balance
  - Required amount
  - Missing amount
  - Quick top-up button
- After a successful payment, automatically close the modal and continue the lead acceptance flow without requiring another click.

### 2. Live lead expiry countdown
Display a real-time countdown:
- On every lead card.
- At the top of the lead detail page.
- Use clear visual states:
  - Normal
  - Less than 1 hour remaining
  - Expired
- Update automatically every second without requiring a page refresh.

### 3. Wallet balance in the navigation
Display the contractor's current wallet balance in the top navigation/header.
Requirements:
- Always visible.
- Updates immediately after any transaction.
- Clicking it opens the Wallet page.

### 4. One-click accept
Add an "Accept" action directly on each lead card.
Requirements:
- Skip opening the detail page.
- If balance is insufficient, trigger the inline top-up modal instead.
- Show loading, success and error states.

## Admin

### 1. Wallet adjustment audit trail (Highest Priority)
Whenever an admin manually credits or debits a wallet:
- Require a reason before confirming.
- Store the reason with the transaction.
- Display it in wallet history and transaction details.

### 2. Global contractor search
Add a fast search bar on the admin dashboard.
Requirements:
- Search by:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Company
- Results should appear instantly while typing.
- Clicking a result opens the contractor profile.

### 3. Lead assignment history
On each lead page, add a timeline showing:
- Contractor offered
- Date/time
- Accepted
- Declined
- Expired
- Current status
Display it as a clean chronological timeline.

### 4. Export contractors
Add an "Export CSV" button on the Contractors page.
Include:
- Name
- Email
- Company
- Wallet balance
- Status
- Registration date
